Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a layered pasta dish

Ingredients

  • minced meat
  • tineed tomato
  • carrot
  • onion
  • celery
  • lasagne sheet
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook carrot, celery, and onion
  2. Add in tin tomato and cook until reduced
  3. Cook lasagne sheets
  4. Layer lasagne sheets, sauce, and cheese in a pan
  5. Bake in oven
  6. Let cool before serving
